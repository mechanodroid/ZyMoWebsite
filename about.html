About ZyMo Entertainment

ZyMo Entertainment, a subsidiary of ZyQuest Inc, is a professional game development company located in Green Bay WI. ZyMo is an experienced team of veterans from companies such as Raven Software (Quake 4, Wolfenstein), Radical Entertainment (Prototype), Treyarch (Call of Duty) and Human Head (Prey2).

ZyMo creates distinctive and engaging interactive experiences. Our expertise is in mobile, web, content creation and providing custom development solutions.

The development group which makes up ZyMo Entertainment has been together for more than five years as Frozen Codebase. The group has shipped a number of games together including Screwjumper!, Elements of Destruction, Kick-Ass, Zombie Wranglers, Scarygirl, Burgertime and more.

ZyMo is broken up into individual teams, each focusing on a different game. Traditionally, each of the teams has focused on downloadable distributable games for Xbox Live Arcade, Play Station Network and WiiWare. The company has recently begun focusing on iOS and Android games and has finished Ninja Feet of Fury for mobile devices. Past clients of ZyMo include Garage Games, THQ, Sierra Online and Konami.

About ZyQuest Inc

ZyQuest was incorporated in January 1995. It is a progressive IT service providing custom software development, IT infrastructure, and IT staffing for it's customers. ZyQuest has started many other ventures in the past and has a related venture-based corporation known as ZyQuest Ventures.
